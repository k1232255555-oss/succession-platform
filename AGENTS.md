## AI Company Release Reviews

Before public copy, pricing, onboarding, or release changes, run these reviews and record the result.

### Brand / Copy / UX Review

- A first-time visitor can understand what the service is and who it is for.
- Copy is respectful, avoids fear-based exaggeration, and matches the Founder intent.
- English terms and internal jargon are minimized in user-facing screens.
- Public pages feel clear, approachable, and trustworthy rather than like investment solicitation or fundraising.
- The next action is visible, truthful, and appropriate for the current release stage.

### Business Model Stress Test

- Identify who pays, when they pay, why they continue paying, and when they may leave.
- Look for workarounds, cancellation timing, refund and dispute risks, and operating burden.
- Compare monthly, fixed-term, success-fee, upfront-fee, deposit, and hybrid models before implementation.
- Separate what must be fixed now from what should wait for user evidence.

### All Button Behavior Check

- Every visible button and link must navigate, submit, copy, or clearly appear disabled.
- Do not ship controls that do nothing when activated.
- Verify copy against implemented behavior, database reality, billing state, and external-service configuration.
- Verify public pages do not expose environment, security, personal, company, candidate, or internal operational information.
- AI Company must report these gaps before the Founder discovers them.
